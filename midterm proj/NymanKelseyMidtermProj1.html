The Yurts at Atlantic Trails

What is a yurt?

Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt has canvas walls a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.

How are the yurts furnished?

Each yurt is furnished with a queen-sized bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. The luxury camping expiernce also includes eletricity an a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.

What should I being?
